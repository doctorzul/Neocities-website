Welcome home!

Manifesto: Must this too pass?

Nothing in this world is permanent, and that's a terrible thing. But it's also a great thing! I love (like most people) to feel sorry for myself when I'm down in the dumps. I do. But I also love to remind myself that this too shall pass. No bad moment is eternal. Of course, that also means that no good moment is eternal, either. So, in order to tip the scales in my favor a little (and because it's good practice and I love it...!), I'm undergoing an effort to catalogue the things that make me happy. The good, the funny, the stupid, the weird, the works. This is that! Thanks for stopping by.
